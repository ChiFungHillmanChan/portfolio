About Baccarat

Baccarat is a card game with three possible outcomes: Player wins, Banker wins, or Tie. The Banker bet has the lowest house edge at ~1.06% (with 5% commission). While card counting has minimal effect on main bets, it can reveal profitable opportunities in Egalité (tie) side bets when specific card distributions occur.

Use the Card Counting mode to track cards and identify advantageous betting situations.
