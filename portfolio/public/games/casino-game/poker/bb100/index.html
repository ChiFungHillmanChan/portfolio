What counts as a good win rate?

Rough benchmarks from cash-game tracking communities:

  • < 0 bb/100 — losing rate; review strategy and game selection.
  • 0–3 bb/100 — marginal winner; variance still dominates.
  • 3–7 bb/100 — solid, sustainable winner at most stakes.
  • 7–15 bb/100 — strong rate, typical of good micro-stakes players.
  • 15+ bb/100 — crushing; usually small samples or microstakes.

Sample matters more than rate. Below 10,000 hands, bb/100 is mostly noise. Serious win-rate claims usually need 50,000–100,000+ hands.
