About bb/100

bb/100 (big blinds per 100 hands) is the standard cash-game win rate metric, because it normalises results across stakes. The formula is simple: (profit ÷ big blind) ÷ hands × 100.

A sustainable winning rate at micros is typically 3–10 bb/100; at mid stakes, even 2–5 bb/100 is excellent. Below 10,000 hands, your measured rate is mostly noise — variance dominates.
