How to Play

Post equal Ante and Blind bets, plus optional side bets. You and the dealer each get two hole cards, sharing five community cards. The earlier you raise, the bigger you can bet: 4x/3x before the flop, 2x on the flop, or 1x after the river — check to wait, but once you raise, you're done betting. Never raised by the river? It's 1x or fold. The dealer needs a pair to qualify; otherwise your Ante pushes.
